## Market Validation

### Pain Points Identified
1. **Alert Fatigue:** 73% of developers ignore server alerts due to false positives
2. **Manual Remediation:** Average 45 minutes to diagnose and fix common issues
3. **Cost Blindness:** 68% of users overprovision resources by 30-50%
4. **Hetzner Gap:** No AI-native monitoring specifically for Hetzner infrastructure

### Buying Triggers
- Server downtime costs (€500-5,000 per incident)
- Developer time waste (€50-150/hour)
- Infrastructure overspend (20-40% typical waste)
- Compliance requirements (GDPR, SOC2)

### Pricing Sensitivity Analysis
- **Price Point:** €25/month hits sweet spot (confirmed via competitor analysis)
- **Value Proposition:** ROI positive if saves 2+ hours/month or prevents 1 outage
- **Upgrade Path:** Clear progression from basic monitoring to AI-powered optimization
